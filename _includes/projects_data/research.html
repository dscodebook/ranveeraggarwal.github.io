Research Projects

Tracking and Reconstruction of a Human Body

Undergraduate Dissertation · Prof. Parag Chaudhuri

We are designing a novel method to reconstruct human body using noisy point cloud data in real-time from a single Kinect. The method is currently being implemented in OpenFrameworks using OpenNI and NiTE. We'll be further investigating methods to render the reconstructed model in virtual reality, viewable on a head mounted display like Oculus.

Traffic Infraction Detection through Mobile Devices

Research and Development Project · Prof. Siddhartha Chaudhuri & Prof. Bhaskaran Raman

We are exploring ways to detect signal jumping and tailgating using a mobile phone mounted on the vehicle dashboard. Crowdsourced data and object detection techniques will be employed to get data from videos in near real-time.
